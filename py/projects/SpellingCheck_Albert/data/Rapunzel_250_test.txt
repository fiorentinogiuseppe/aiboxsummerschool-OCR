It was, however, surrounded by a bigb wall, and no one dared
to go into it because it belonged to an enchantress, who had great power
and was dreaded by all the world. One day the woman was standing by this
window and looking down into the garden, when she saw a bed which was
planted with the most beautiful rampion (rapunzel), and it looked so fresh
and green that she longed for it, she quite pined away, and began to look pale
and miserable.
